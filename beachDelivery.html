Where do you deliver?
We deliver to 24 public beach access points in Olde Naples — from 7th Ave N and North Lake Drive south to 33rd Ave S, including Central Ave and Broad Ave S.
How do I find the closest access?
Tap Use my location on the home screen or in Account. We'll pick the nearest open beach access based on your GPS.
How do I change my drop-off spot?
Go to Account to set a preferred access, or tap the access name in the header to choose from all downtown Naples beach accesses.
Can I cancel an order?
Contact us within 2 minutes of placing your order and we'll do our best to cancel before preparation starts.
Need more help?
Email hello@beachdelivery.com or text (555) 000-1234.
