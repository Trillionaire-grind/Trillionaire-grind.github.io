The "Amazon Tax"

Students are losing, and so is the planet.

→ The Financial Drain: Students spend over $1,000 on books that sit on shelves gathering dust after 15 weeks.
→ The Waste: Amazon shipping creates a massive carbon footprint for books that are already physically on campus.
→ The Friction: Buying used books online involves shipping delays, "wrong edition" risks, and high seller fees.

The Solution

A Private, Verified P2P Marketplace.

✓ Green Book creates a closed-loop economy exclusive to each campus.
✓ Minimum Effort: Students simply upload their schedule; the app automatically matches them with peers on campus who have those exact books.
✓ Maximum Trust: Requires a verified school email. Transactions happen person-to-person.

How It Works

From Schedule to Syllabus in 3 Clicks.

Sync

Student connects their course schedule.

Match

Identify every student on campus selling those specific titles.

Exchange

Meet on campus. No shipping, no packaging, no 30% middleman fee.

The "Unfair" Advantage

Zero Overhead = Infinite Value.

No Inventory: We don't own books.

No Logistics: No warehouses, no Amazon trucks, no shipping costs.

True Market Price: Because our costs are near zero, students keep 100% of the value. We even facilitate "free" book hand-me-downs—something Amazon would never allow.

The "Green" in Green Book

Sustainability through Circularity.

  • Extending Life: Incentivizing local reselling extends the physical life of a textbook.
  • Reducing Carbon: Eliminating "Last Mile" delivery for thousands of heavy books.
  • University Goals: Helps the school meet its ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) targets.

The Business Model

Enterprise SaaS for Education.

We don’t charge students fees. We don’t take a cut of the sale.

The Client: The University.

The Value: Schools pay a yearly contract to provide Green Book as a premium student benefit—a powerful recruitment and retention tool.
