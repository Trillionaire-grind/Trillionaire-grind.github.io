If you've ever felt something for someone you weren't supposed to… this book was written for you.

LaShana comes from a family with a long history in the hotel business — her grandparents worked in hotels, and her parents met while working at the same hotel. When she launched her own hotel, she was carrying that legacy forward.

Driven to grow, she joins Women in Entrepreneurship, run by Tech & Tech, the hottest startup in the city. There she meets Michael — handsome, charismatic CEO, and completely off-limits as her mentor.

As mentor and mentee, they work together to grow their businesses. But it's not only their businesses that are growing — their attraction to each other is too. Although they try to keep it secret, secrets have a way of surfacing.

Will their hidden attraction get in the way of business — or build something greater?
