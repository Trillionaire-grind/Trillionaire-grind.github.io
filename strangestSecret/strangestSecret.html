30 Day Challenge

  1. Write on a card what you want more than anything else-a single, clearly defined goal
  2. Think of it each day with optimism. Remember you must become what you think about, it's the law.
  3. Stop thinking about what it is you fear, replace it with a positive thought-control your thinking
  4. Do more than you have to
  5. Do of yourself more
  6. Don't concern yourself with how you are going to achieve the goal, just where you are going. The answers will come to you.
