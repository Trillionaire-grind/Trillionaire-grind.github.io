Q1 2026 — Mangé Lakay update

Reporting period: January – March 2026 · Published April 2026

Summary: Mangé Lakay continued daily boiled-egg sales in Kotfè with a ~30% margin. We maintained 45 laying hens and 5 roosters on 5 centièmes of rented land. Side businesses (phone top-up and charge-your-electronics) covered feed costs. No new major capital deployment this quarter — focus was steady operations and documenting the path to scale.
45 laying hens
5 centièmes under management

Operations

  • Product: Boiled eggs — popular breakfast item in Kotfè.
  • Supply constraint: Local market still could not supply more than ~100 eggs/day for a city of 50,000+ — confirming why we must grow our own flock.
  • Land: 5 centièmes rented through October 2033; ~10 centièmes expected via inheritance (not yet in the fund).
  • Side revenue: Mobile top-up and charge-your-electronics services continued to offset feed and daily operating costs.
