Role overview

Ah-Counter
Notes overused words and filler sounds, then reports at the end of the meeting.
Grammarian
Helps members improve grammar and vocabulary, including the word of the day.
Timer
Tracks speech, table topics, and evaluation times for qualified voting.
Toastmaster
Hosts the meeting and keeps the agenda moving smoothly.
Table Topics Master
Leads impromptu speaking with short prompts for members.
Evaluator
Gives structured verbal and written feedback to speakers.
General Evaluator
Evaluates the meeting flow and club helper reports.
