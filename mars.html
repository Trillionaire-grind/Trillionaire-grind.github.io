Who is BettrLife Financial?

Our Story

Mars and Ralph Desire began their journey in the financial world in 2021 with a simple mission — to serve people and learn everything they could about how money truly works. What started as two brothers determined to make a difference quickly grew into a purpose-driven firm helping educators, families, and retirees throughout Florida make wiser decisions about their wealth.

The brothers saw firsthand how many families were left unsure about retirement, taxes, and estate planning. They decided to build something different — a firm grounded in faith, stewardship, and education. BettrLife Financial Services was born with the belief that financial planning should serve your purpose, not the other way around.

FRS Services

Florida Retirement System guidance with confidence

Helping you understand your Florida Retirement System benefits and turn them into a confident retirement strategy. Whether you participate in the Pension Plan or the Investment Plan, knowing how your benefits fit into your overall plan is an important step.

Pension Plan vs Investment Plan

A side-by-side review of pension income and investment strategies to help understand how each option may support long-term retirement goals.

2nd Election review & timing

An overview of second-election options and how timing decisions may affect future retirement benefits.

DROP vs no-DROP analysis

A comparison of participating in DROP versus a traditional retirement path to evaluate potential long-term outcomes.

Pension estimate verification

A review of pension estimates to help confirm accuracy and better understand projected retirement benefits.

DROP participation strategy

An overview of how participating in DROP may impact retirement timing, benefit accumulation, and overall financial planning.

DROP tax & exit-year income planning

A review of potential tax considerations related to DROP distributions, plus income sources during the transition year into retirement.
