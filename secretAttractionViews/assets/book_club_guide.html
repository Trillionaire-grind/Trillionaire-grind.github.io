Book Club Discussion Guide

A Secret Attraction Between Mentor and Mentee

About the characters

  1. LaShana carries a hotel legacy from her grandparents and parents. How does that history shape the choices she makes — in business and in love?
  2. What did you notice about Michael in his first scenes? What makes him a good leader, and what are his flaws?
  3. Are LaShana and Michael equally driven, or does one sacrifice more for the relationship? Discuss.

Theme & tension

  1. The story plays with a forbidden dynamic: mentor and mentee. How else could the characters have handled it?
  2. How do ambition and attraction compete in this story? Can both win, or must one lose?
  3. Secrets are a major theme. Which secrets hurt the most — the ones kept from others, or the ones kept from yourself?

Business & legacy

  1. What does Women in Entrepreneurship mean for Michael and his moral dilemma?
  2. How does the hotel world contrast with the tech startup world in the story? What does each setting bring to the romance?
  3. By the end, do you believe their businesses and their feelings can coexist? Why or why not?

Wrap-up

  1. Which scene stayed with you the longest after you closed the book?
  2. Who would you cast in a film adaptation — and why?
  3. If you could ask the author one question, what would it be?
