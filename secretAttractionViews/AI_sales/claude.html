If you've ever felt something for someone you weren't supposed to… this book was written for you.

LaShana comes from a family with a long, proud history in the hotel business. Her grandparents worked in hotels. Her parents met while working at the same hotel. It wasn't just a career — it was a calling. And when LaShana launched her own hotel, she was carrying that legacy forward.

But LaShana is ambitious. She wants more. So when she hears about "Women in Entrepreneurship" — an exclusive mentorship program run by Tech & Tech, the hottest startup in the city — she applies without hesitation.

She's there to grow her business. That's all. That's the plan.

Then she meets Michael.

Michael is the CEO of Tech & Tech. He's handsome, charismatic, sharp in a boardroom — and completely off-limits. He's her mentor. He's helping her build something. Getting involved would risk everything they're both working toward.

So they keep their distance. They stay professional. They tell themselves the pull they feel is just mutual respect. Just admiration. Just business.

But secrets have a way of surfacing. And some attractions are too strong to stay hidden.
