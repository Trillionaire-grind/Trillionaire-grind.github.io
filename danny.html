Meet Danny

A Passion for Baking, Rooted in Naples

What started as a childhood love of his grandmother's kitchen has grown into Naples' favorite neighborhood bakery. Danny brings over 8 years of artisan baking experience to every item that comes out of his oven.

🌾
Locally sourced ingredients We partner with Florida farms for the freshest butter, eggs, and produce.
⏰
Baked before sunrise, every day Danny starts at 4am so everything is warm and fresh when you arrive.
🎂
No preservatives. Ever. Real butter, real flour, real flavor. Nothing artificial in our kitchen.
